### Tip 1) Focus On Common Words!

*Rare words should be optional*

#### Why?

- When a player doesn't find an answer, it then influences perception of the puzzle!
  - Also why having a team to test is important so some will view words as more common or not!

### Tip 2) Offer Clues via Gameplay

#### Why?

- It can be demoralizing to players to use hints

#### Some non-hint clues are...

- Themes
- Traps
  - Showing players what it's not
     - Think Wordle (green, yellow, gray)
- Gradually as they progress

#### Fun Fact

When it comes to Wordle, there are general responses based on the number of guesses!

|Guesses|Player Response|
|--|--|
|1st|Luck|
|2nd-4th|Skill|
|+5|Loss|

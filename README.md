## Intro

So, hey y'all! My name is Edward Leon, and I was the lucky few that was able to attend GDC this time around (2026).

To say GDC was filled with a plethora of learning opportunities would be an understatement.

Entering GDC 2026 I wanted to be a sponge since at the time of writing this, I'm a recent graduate from CSUMB and am getting a game dev group together. Needless to say, that I may have went to a fair amount of talks centered on funding, leadership, and content creation!

As for a final note, me being at GDC 2026, and in turn, the creation of this repository, would not have been possible without the folks at LatinX in Gaming!

That being said, if you have the time, please do check out all the work that they do! They're an amazing non-profit that has done wonders to support the game dev community as a whole!

## Topic: Community Safety

> **NOTE:** As a non-profit, LatinX in Gaming has to stay politically neutral! Straying from this would result in the organization in losing it's non-profit status!

### General Recommendations from the Audience:

#### When Traveling...

- Keep passports on hand
- Either...
  - Travel in Groups
  - Act like you're in a group
- Use Discord or other trusted social groups and let the know of travel plans

> Misc pitch: Using virtual experiences to connect game devs world wide!

#### As for general knowledge of current circumstances...

The huge problem is that there are a lot of uneducated assumptions!

*Solution: Just spread more info about current news!*

> A non-profit, whilst not allowed to take a stance, can repost news or inform it's audience on social media!

> *For instance, a non-profit could inform it's community when new ICE encampments pop up!*

- Potentially risky given the current government...

## Topic: Funding & Investment

### Notes from LatinX in Gaming

#### Grants

They were available/previously available!

- Up to $1,000 per dev/team

#### Other notes

The organization does have connections to get developers Steam Deck dev kits!

### Notes from the audience

#### The Coldest Winter team/dev mentioned...

**DEVS NEED TO THINK ABOUT WHAT PUBLISHERS WANT!!!**

(*exaggerated by me as this is a common takeaway*)

Publishers want:

- More wishlists
- More engagement
- More of the game (done)
  - Not just the idea of a game...

#### More notes from the audience

##### Pitch: Progress Jam

How it would work:

- Clear video/representation of where a game is before start of jam
- Devs work on WIP project
- Devs then submit and show progress made within the time of the jam!

##### In the education space...

- There should be some club outreach
  - Many students want to here from game dev orgs!

In regards to teaching specifically:

- Game dev should be tied further with STEM to legitimatize it
- Teach kids whilst they're young!
- Helps uplift students (*by teaching them new ways to practice STEM skills*)
- Parents will pay for their kids to learn about game development!

##### Some more notes from the audience...

- Most may not know currency conversions, as well as how much money is worth in whatever country
  - So reasons for that would be great!

> Think of the Big Mac index!
